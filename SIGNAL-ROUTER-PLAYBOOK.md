## WHAT THE ROUTER IS FOR

Your chart-eye already reads price, momentum, MAs, trend. The router exists to give you the **three things your chart can't show you in one glance**:

1. **Cascade** — are alts confirming BTC or breaking away?
2. **Cycle** — where are we against the March 29 / $65,968 trough by day count?
3. **Rates** — is macro putting a ceiling on crypto?

The other four fragments (momentum, pillars, trend, quiet) re-state what your eyes already see. Treat them as padding. The composite score is also padding. **The signal lives in the conflict list, the cascade detail, the cycle day, and the rates line.** That's it.

Edge from PLAYBOOK.md is still the trade. Router is the institutional cross-check that tells you when to size up, size down, or stand aside.

---

## DAILY READ — 30 SECONDS, IN THIS ORDER

1. **Conflict list** — anything firing? If yes, that's your whole read.
2. **Cascade detail** — which alts are at 0/5 or 1/5? Which are at 4/5 or 5/5?
3. **Cycle day** — what number of the 60-day cycle are we on?
4. **Rates line** — contained, elevated, or breakout?
5. **Final signal** — only useful as a sanity check that the above agree.

If the conflict list is empty and cascade/rates are quiet, the router has nothing to add. Trade your pattern. If the conflict list is loud, **the router is the senior voice in the room** — slow down, re-read your setup with that context.

## CONFLICT LIST — THE GEM

Each conflict the router can fire, what it means, what you do.

| Conflict fires | What it actually means | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **MIXED — fragments disagree** | Six fragments split 3-3. Market has no consensus. No one is in control. | **Sit out.** This is the genuine no-edge state. Don't manufacture a trade. |
| **CYCLE vs MOMENTUM** | Cycle says we're in a buy window but price isn't responding yet. Either Bressert anchor is stale or momentum is about to flip up. | **Wait for momentum.** Don't front-run the cycle alone. If momentum confirms in next 3-5 days, that's your entry. |
| **PILLARS vs TREND — short-term only** | Short-term setup looks bullish but the larger trend is broken. Scalp setup, not swing. | **If you take it, take it small.** Quick targets, tight stop. Don't let a scalp become a position trade. |
| **CASCADE vs MOMENTUM** | BTC is grinding up but alts are leaving the party. Classic late-cycle topping pattern — money flowing out of the speculative end while BTC absorbs last. | **Stop adding. Lock gains.** This is the institutional distribution tell. BTC strength here is borrowed. |
| **RATES vs CASCADE — ceiling risk** | Crypto wants to run but ^TNX or ^TYX is breaking out. The bid is fake — institutional money won't let crypto rip while yields are climbing. | **Sell into strength.** Don't chase. Any rip is a fade target until rates settle. |
| **FAKE BREAKOUT detected** | Price hugging VWAP on low volume. The move isn't real — no participation behind it. | **Pass.** No edge either way. Wait for volume to confirm direction. |
| **N alts below 200W** | Multiple alts have broken the 200-week — the cycle floor. Weakest links breaking first is how cycles end. | **Exit alts immediately. Reduce BTC.** When 3+ alts are sub-200W, BTC is borrowing time. Get small. |

## CASCADE STATE — WHERE THE RISK CURVE IS

The cascade is the most institutional fragment. It tells you whether speculative capital is flowing toward risk or away from it.

| Cascade state | What's happening | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **RISK ON — full expansion** | BTC + 4+ alts above 4 of 5 MAs each; 3+ alts outperforming BTC on 30D ROC. Full bull conditions. | **Add risk.** Lean alts. This is the rare window where alt selection matters more than BTC direction. |
| **RISK ON (cautious)** | Partial expansion. Some alts cooperating, some not. Healthy bull but rotation is uneven. | **Hold positions, don't add aggressively.** Watch which alts are breaking — that's where the rotation is going. |
| **MIXED** | No clear direction. Neither risk on nor risk off. | **Don't initiate.** Manage what you have. |
| **RISK OFF (leaning)** | Alts starting to crack across multiple MAs. Defensive posture forming. | **Trim alts. Don't add to anything.** BTC may hold longer but the curve is rolling. |
| **RISK OFF** | Alts breaking down hard. Capital fleeing the speculative end. | **Exit alts. BTC at risk.** This usually leads BTC tops by 1-4 weeks. Cash is a position. |

**Read the cascade detail line, not just the label.** `BTC:5/5 | ETH:5/5 | SOL:4/5 | XRP:2/5 | TIA:1/5 | TRX:5/5` tells you which alts are leading and which are dying. XRP and TIA cracking while BTC is intact = the leak is starting. That's actionable before the cascade label changes.

## CYCLE DAY — WHERE YOU ARE

Cycle runs 60 days from trough to trough. March 29, 2026 = day 0 / $65,968. **Never update the trough date without flagging it first.**

| Cycle day | Label | What's happening | What you do |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 0-18** | EARLY CYCLE | Best risk/reward window. Bottom is fresh, upside is most of the cycle. | **Add aggressively.** Size full per edge. This is where annual P&L is made. |
| **Day 18-36** | MID CYCLE | Trend is established. Cycle is producing returns. | **Ride.** Hold. Don't chase late entries. |
| **Day 36-51** | LATE CYCLE | Trend exhausting. Distribution may be starting. | **Tighten stops. Trim winners.** Lock gains. Don't add to longs. |
| **Day 51-66** | TROUGH WINDOW | Next trough is forming. The bounce is coming but timing is fuzzy ±3 days. | **Watch for the entry, don't catch the knife.** Wait for edge or pattern to confirm the new cycle has started. |
| **Day 66+** | EXTENDED | Cycle is overdue. Either it extended (rare but happens) or your trough anchor is wrong. | **Lower conviction.** Don't trust cycle reads until the trough resets. Trade purely on edge + pattern. |

## RATES STATE — THE MACRO LID

10Y (^TNX) and 30Y (^TYX) yields tell you whether crypto can breathe.

| Rates state | What's happening | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| **RATES OK** | Yields below 20D and 50D averages. Bond market quiet. | **Macro green light.** Crypto can run without a lid. Trade direction. |
| **RATES NEUTRAL** | Mixed signal. Yields not pressing higher but not falling. | **Default mode.** No macro tailwind, no macro headwind. |
| **RATES ELEVATED** | Yields sustained above 20D + 50D. Trending up but not yet at 52W highs. | **Cap your enthusiasm.** ATH breakouts in crypto are unlikely while yields are climbing. Take faster profits. |
| **RATES BREAKOUT** | 10Y or 30Y at or near 52-week highs. Institutional duration trade is on. | **Risk-off ceiling is live.** Crypto loses its bid. Reduce risk regardless of what the chart says. Don't fight rates. |

**The mechanism:** when long yields break out, institutional money rotates out of duration and out of risk simultaneously — bonds get sold AND crypto gets sold to raise cash. Crypto can grind sideways under this pressure but not break out. Until rates settle (yields back inside the 20D/50D), crypto rallies are fade targets.

## COMPOSITE SIGNAL — MOSTLY IGNORE

The router outputs STRONG BUY / BUY BIAS / NEUTRAL / SELL BIAS / STRONG SELL based on the weighted composite. **You can mostly ignore this.** It's the average of the seven fragments and it smooths over the fragment-level information that matters.

If you do glance at it, use it only as a sanity check:

| Composite | Sanity check |
|---|---|
| **STRONG BUY** (>1.2) | Edge should also be saying BUY. If edge says EXIT and router says STRONG BUY, **trust edge** — the composite is averaging fragments that lag your chart. |
| **BUY BIAS** (0.5 to 1.2) | Tilt long if cascade/rates/cycle agree. Half-size if they don't. |
| **NEUTRAL** (-0.5 to 0.5) | The router has no conviction. Trade pattern + edge only. |
| **SELL BIAS** (-1.2 to -0.5) | Tighten. Don't add. Match edge if edge is trimming. |
| **STRONG SELL** (<-1.2) | Get out of alts at minimum. Reduce BTC unless edge says hold. |

**Whenever composite disagrees with edge, edge wins.** PLAYBOOK.md is empirically validated; the router weights are not.

## QUICK FIELD REFERENCE — WORST-CASE READS

If the dashboard is flashing one of these, read it as a stop-and-think:

- **`RATES BREAKOUT` + cascade `RISK OFF`** → top is in or close. Cash. Wait.
- **`3+ alts below 200W`** → alt cycle is over. Sell alts even if BTC is fine. Reduce BTC by half.
- **`CASCADE vs MOMENTUM` + cycle day > 50** → classic distribution. Don't chase BTC. Trim into strength.
- **`FAKE BREAKOUT` + low volume** → ignore the price move entirely. Wait for real participation.
- **Cycle `EXTENDED` (day 66+)** → trough is stale. Re-anchor the trough manually before trusting any cycle read. Flag it in the chat.

If the dashboard is flashing one of these, you can lean in:

- **Cascade `RISK ON — full expansion` + Rates `OK` + cycle day < 18** → loaded gun. Size up per edge. This is when the year is made.
- **Empty conflict list + edge BUY** → router has no objection to your trade. Full conviction.

## CADENCE

- **Every entry/exit decision:** scan conflict list + cascade detail. 15 seconds. If clean, trade edge. If loud, slow down.
- **Daily:** check cycle day. Adjust default size lean based on early/mid/late.
- **Weekly:** review fragment behavior vs price. Where did the router warn early? Where did it miss? Adjust which fragments you weight in your read.

The router is your institutional second opinion. It doesn't trade for you. It tells you what Chris sees automatically and you have to read deliberately. Over time, you'll internalize these patterns and the doc becomes optional.

Until then: open this when the conflict list fires, find the row, take the action.
